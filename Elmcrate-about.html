Graphic • Web + Portrait Artist - Cara Steffen

BIO

I am a wife, mother, sister, daughter and friend. I grew up on a farm in the northeast tip of Kansas and still today find it to be a place of beauty and rest. My first job was milking cows on our dairy when I was in 6th grade through high school. The advantage of waking up at 4 in the morning was breathing in the fresh crisp air and witnessing the quiet beauty of the sun-rising. It became one of my favorite times of the day. With all that said, I am so grateful God directed our family to Colorado. The beauty is unrivaled, the weather amazing and the journey has developed patience, character and strength I've never before known. I am honest, kind, optimistic, a hard worker and desire to keep on learning. I look forward to working with you.
